MOMENTUM CONTINUITYEvery rig morph inherits the previous form's position and linear velocity. Returning to a wheeled state automatically rights the chassis while preserving motion.
MORPH THE SCENEALL RIGID, ALL RAGDOLL, ALL JELLY, and MIXED rebuild the current test population at sampled positions, making direct material comparisons possible.

GENERATIVE SOUNDA local Web Audio instrument produces slow ambient tones, close-up ASMR impacts, movement hum, and material-specific morph sweeps. Audio requires one user gesture.
WORLD MATERIALSSTEEL, RUBBER, ICE, FOAM, and MOON alter gravity, friction, restitution, roughness, visual response, and acoustic space across the arena.
CONTROL INTENTWASD / arrows and touch are normalized into desired velocity, heading, throttle, steer, brake, jump, and ability. The input layer never mentions wheels, joints, or vertices.
CAMERAA fixed high oblique orthographic camera follows the active form. Drag the world horizontally to rotate the testing table.
SOFT ADAPTERThe jelly car derives center, velocity, contact confidence, and deformation from its vertices. Ground vertices receive traction; left/right vertex fields steer; pressure and shape memory preserve the car form.
ARTICULATED ADAPTERThe ragdoll car uses Jolt constraint motors to preserve a loose vehicle pose while traction and braking are distributed across its grounded parts.

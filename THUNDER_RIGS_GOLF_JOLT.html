JOLT CAR GOLF

Tap the ball and the physical vehicle finds the back of it, lines up with the cup, accelerates, and makes contact.

Drag from the ball to choose a bank, gap, bumper, or ramp line. Drag length controls the rig's run-up speed.

Tap a ramp and the rig approaches its low edge, locks to the corridor, climbs on Jolt suspension, and releases after the lip.

Tap turf or a bumper to drive there. WASD / arrows temporarily override the automated driver. Space brakes. Shift boosts.

The car, ball, walls, bumpers, ramps, suspension, gravity, friction, restitution, and impacts are simulated in Jolt. The camera remains a high oblique top-down 3D view.

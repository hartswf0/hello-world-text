MORPH THE RIGRIGID is a hard wheeled chassis. ELASTIC adds recoverable skin deformation. RAGDOLL replaces the car with a jointed modular chassis. JELLY replaces it with a tetrahedral soft-body car.
MOMENTUM CONTINUITYEvery rig morph inherits the previous form's position and linear velocity. Returning to a wheeled state automatically rights the chassis while preserving motion.
MORPH THE SCENEALL RIGID, ALL RAGDOLL, ALL JELLY, and MIXED rebuild the current test population at sampled positions, making direct material comparisons possible.
WORLD MATERIALSSTEEL, RUBBER, ICE, FOAM, and MOON alter gravity, friction, restitution, roughness, and visual response across the test arena.
CONTROLSWASD / arrows or the left stick. BRAKE damps the active physical form. BOOST and JUMP apply impulses to whichever ontology currently embodies the rig.
CAMERAA fixed high oblique orthographic camera follows the active form. Drag the world horizontally to rotate the testing table.
SOFT BODYThe jelly car and cubes use edge and volume constraints, internal pressure, collision, and live vertex transfer from Jolt into Three.js.

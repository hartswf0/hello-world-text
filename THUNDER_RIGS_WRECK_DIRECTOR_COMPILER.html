MATERIAL CONSEQUENCE ENGINE

MORPH THE RIGRIGID is a hard wheeled chassis. ELASTIC adds recoverable skin deformation. RAGDOLL replaces the car with a jointed modular chassis. JELLY replaces it with a tetrahedral soft-body car.
MOMENTUM CONTINUITYEvery rig morph inherits the previous form's position and linear velocity. Returning to a wheeled state automatically rights the chassis while preserving motion.
MORPH THE SCENEALL RIGID, ALL RAGDOLL, ALL JELLY, and MIXED rebuild the current test population at sampled positions, making direct material comparisons possible.
PAIR MATERIALSEach body keeps its own material. The contact listener combines the two materials at impact instead of repainting every body with one global preset.
AIMDrag from the rig toward the intended impact. The line forecasts the run. Press FILM SHOT to record and launch.
MATERIAL SOUNDImpacts, skids, fractures, rubber, gel, mud, and engine motion are synthesized with Web Audio. Recorded takes include the same sound track.
CORRECTDuring a filmed run, drag once to add a small course correction. Use REWIND after the take to restore the clean start.
WORLD CONSEQUENCESteel dents. Glass and ice shatter. Wood splinters. Concrete cracks. Foam crushes. Rubber rebounds. Mud and asphalt retain tracks. Every event enters the ledger.

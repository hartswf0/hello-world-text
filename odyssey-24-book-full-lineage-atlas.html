Theory of the atlas

These are not chapter summaries. Each book is treated as a formation ecology: inherited sources, origin questions, disciplinary or technical matrix, weighted epochs, migrations, evidence, risks, a jukebox of decisive precedents, a signature ecology, and one research or build echo. Literature lineages govern interpretive and historical chapters; code/math lineages govern state, verification, control, and formal risk; R&D lineages govern prototypes, institutions, adoption, and deployment.

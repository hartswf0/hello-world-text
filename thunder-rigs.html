THUNDER RIGS · MODES + WRECK STUDIO

THREE MODES, NEVER A PANELDRIVE, BUILD, REVIEW — switch at top-right. Exactly one is active; the world is never covered. Each mode shows only a thin bar at the bottom edge.
DRIVEJust the stick, pads, and gauges. Clean windshield. This is the ground state.
BUILDOne bottom bar: pick a body kind, tap SPAWN, it drops into the space you're looking at. Sliders tune the next spawn. Tap a body in the world to select it (cyan ring); the SPAWN button becomes RESPAWN to retune a selected jelly.
REVIEW — FILM A WRECKThe sim records a rolling 10-second buffer of the action. In REVIEW, time freezes and a timeline appears. Scrub to replay the wreck, set IN / OUT marks, then EXPORT the clip.
CAPTURE MODESROLLING = always recording the last 10s. Tap REC to switch to ARMED: press once to start a fresh take, again to stop, then scrub that take. Rolling is lighter on memory; armed captures a clean run.
EXPORT: WEBM OR PNGEXPORT renders IN→OUT to a WebM video if your device supports it, otherwise it falls back automatically to a PNG frame burst. Both are bounded and safe on low-memory devices.
